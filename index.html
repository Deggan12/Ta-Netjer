Our Heritage

What is
Ta Netjer?

☥

For the ancient Egyptians, Ta-Netjer meant "God's Land" - a name also given to the Land of Punt, believed to stretch from present-day Eritrea to Somalia. A journey to this region was akin to a pilgrimage: as extraordinary, in their time, as a trip to the moon might seem today.

They believed this land to be not only the cradle of humanity but also a place of renewal - an opportunity to revitalize through the abundance of nature's riches. Each expedition returned with boats laden with raw materials and local goods essential to life in Pharaoh's Egypt. These voyages were not merely economic missions; they were adventures, filled with stories, discoveries, and achievements worth passing down through generations.

When Queen Hatshepsut sent her expeditions to Punt, the aim was not conquest. Their exchanges were grounded in mutual benefit and respect - the spirit of fair trade written into the ancient world.

Care That Honours
Nature and Heritage

☥
I

God's Land

Mother Earth is not the "Man's World" sung by James Brown. It exists beyond cold steel and industrialization, rooted in a deeply spiritual realm. The Earth belongs to the collective spirit of all living beings - not just humans, and certainly not any single nation.

II

Shared Responsibility

To call it "God's Land" is to recognize the planet as a shared responsibility. There are no true boundaries that justify exploitation or destruction. Territoriality implies exclusivity - and in doing so, it undermines inclusiveness.

III

Fair Exchange

The philosophy of Ta-Netjer embraces fair trade. When ancient expeditions journeyed to Punt, their aim was not conquest, nor exclusive dominance. Their exchanges were based on mutual benefit and respect - a model we carry forward today.

IV

A Living Mission

Each visit to this site is intended to be both educational and meaningful - offering insights into natural living alongside locally handcrafted skincare products. We believe in using only what we need, and caring for future generations above all else.

"Reviving the name Ta-Netjer is an effort to reconnect with a time when life was more harmonious, balanced, and aligned with nature."

- The Ta-Netjer Spirit
